Stand-up comedy


Stand-up comedy is a comedy performance to a live audience, addressed directly from the stage. The performer is known as a comic, comedian, or simply stand-up. Stand-up comedy consists of one-liners, stories or observations that may incorporate props, music or magic tricks. Stand-up comedy can be performed almost anywhere, including but not limited to clubs, festivals, bars, nightclubs, colleges or theatres. Stand-up comedy is the art of telling funny jokes.
